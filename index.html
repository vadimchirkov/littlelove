I am an English nurse with over a decade of experience specialising in childcare and child development from ages 0 to 5 years

I believe that the first few years of child’s life helps to shape the adults we become. Learning how to support families during this time has become my passion.

There are very few things which will change a person’s life in the same way becoming a parent will, which is why I think it is important to find as many tools and support systems as we can to help make this a smooth transition for all the family. Along with this I like to bring fun and laughter to the experience.

Baby Massage Classes
Baby massage classes across ibiza is an opportunity for parents and care givers to learn massage techniques to use with their child. The techniques involve therapeutic strokes on the baby’s skin - don’t worry it is easy to pick up and the benefits are endless. Massage classes are aimed for babies aged 6 months - when they start crawling (by this point it is often difficult to keep them in one spot long enough to endure the classes)
